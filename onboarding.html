Welcome to CipherSheet
CipherSheet allows you to encrypt individual cells in Google Sheets. Only people with the correct .vaultkey file can read or update the cell.

To start, generate a new encryption key by clicking Generate new key in the sidebar. This will instantly download a key file to your computer.
Protecting a Cell
With the key loaded into the Add-on, select any empty cell in your sheet. Type the secret text you want to securely save into the CipherSheet text box, and click Protect.

The cell in the spreadsheet will be replaced with unintelligible ciphertext.
Unprotecting a Cell
Select an encrypted cell, and the sidebar will show Unprotect. Click it to reveal the plaintext.
Note: Unprotecting a cell will permanently write the plaintext to the sheet's history. To simply clear the cell, you can select "Clear cell" without revealing the contents.
Keep Your Key Safe
Because CipherSheet uses zero-knowledge encryption, if you lose your key file, your data cannot be recovered!

Never upload your key file to public places. Instead, share it directly and securely with your trusted collaborators. The key never leaves your local browser — it is never sent to any servers - not Google nor any other servers.
